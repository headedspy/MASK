About the product

If you've ever played Ubisoft's WATCH_DOGS 2 chances are you found a
particular character's mask pretty interesting.We're talking of course
about Wrench's high-tech mask. Its main feature? Change the emote on
the front based on thecharacter's own emote. So how were we able to
create the same thing in the real world?

Distinguishing facial expressions

When making an expression the shape of your whole face changes
more or less. But by sticking to a fixed number of points and
tracking their movements we can distinguish with a pretty high
accuracy the face or even the emotion of the person. The points
we've chosen are the two eyebrows and the mouth. By finding
their state we then compare it to an already created mapping
between expression and position.
